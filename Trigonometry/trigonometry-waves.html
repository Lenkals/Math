Sine — sin θ
Opposite ÷ Hypotenuse
vertical height of rod tip
Green bar = how high the tip sits. Ranges −1 → +1. Zero at 0° & 180°, peak +1 at 90°, trough −1 at 270°.
Cosine — cos θ
Adjacent ÷ Hypotenuse
horizontal reach of rod tip
Blue bar = how far right the tip reaches. Same shape as sine but 90° ahead. Crosses zero at 90° & 270°.
Tangent — tan θ
sin θ ÷ cos θ
intercept on x = 1 tangent line
Where the extended rod meets x = 1. Races to ±∞ at 90° & 270° — the vertical asymptotes.
Secant — sec θ
1 ÷ cos θ
hypotenuse ÷ adjacent
Reciprocal of cosine. Always ≥1 or ≤−1. U-arcs touch cosine peaks. Undefined where cos θ = 0.
Cosecant — csc θ
1 ÷ sin θ
hypotenuse ÷ opposite
Reciprocal of sine, offset 90°. Undefined at 0° & 180°. Its arcs cradle every sine peak and trough.
