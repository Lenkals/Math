Standard basis (before)

e₁ = (1, 0, 0)
e₂ = (0, 1, 0)

Transformed columns (after)

M·e₁ = col 1
M·e₂ = col 2
M·e₃ = col 3
In 3D the same rule holds: M × e₁ = column 1, M × e₂ = column 2, M × e₃ = column 3. The unit cube (formed by the three basis vectors) gets stretched into a parallelepiped. Its volume = |det(M)|.
